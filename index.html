Global Warming

The planet is facing extreme warming, with global temperatures reaching alarming levels. These changes in climate and environment directly impact all living beings, endangering biodiversity and our quality of life.

Global warming poses a direct threat to human health and safety. Intense heat waves, water shortages, and increased natural disasters bring immediate dangers, with growing impact over the coming decades.

We are facing a potential future without humans. Extinction is a real threat if actions to reduce carbon emissions and preserve ecosystems are not intensified. We must act before it’s too late.

Combating this environmental catastrophe requires commitment. Transitioning to renewable energy, reducing carbon emissions, and encouraging sustainability are some urgent actions to protect our future.
